Pin Messages

Pin Messages

Pin important Messages in the Channel to keep newly added Members up-to-date.

### Pin a Message

Desktop

  • Hover over the Message and Click the manage icon.

  • Click [Pin Message].

  • Select the Message you want to Pin, and click [confirm]. All Members of the Channel will be able to see and remove any Pinned Messages.

### View Pinned Messages

Desktop

  • Click the Pin icon at the top of the Channel.

  • You will see a list of all the Pinned Messages in that Channel. Clicking a Pinned Message takes you to the original location of the Message.

### Unpin Messages

Desktop

  • Click the Pin icon at the top of the Channel.

  • Hover over the Message you wish to unpin.

  • Click the delete icon.
