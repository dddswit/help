# Manage Ideas

 ### Edit an Idea



**Dekstop** 

* Click the ![Build a new workspace](https://files.swit.io/help_image/GS_06_Manage_icon.png) Manage icon.


* Click [Edit post].


* Edit the content and click [Confirm].
  The modified Idea will be marked as edited.
